Brief About The Founder

- After graduating from King Fahd University of Petroleum and Minerals in 1398 AH, engineer Muhammad bin Abdullah Al Guwaihes began his professional career as an engineer at the Industrial Research and Development Center in Al-Dar Saudi Arabia. For consulting services in the period from 1398 AH to 1402 AH. Then he took the position of general manager Projects and maintenance at the Ministry of Health for the period from 1402 AH to 1418 AH.


- In 1419 AH, he served as Director General of the Water and Sanitation Authority, and in the same year He also served as a member of several committees and administrative councils, including the Water and Eskimos Committee Public utilities and the Saudi-Japanese Joint Committee.


- After that, Engineer Al Guwaihes worked as a member of the Shura Council from 1422 AH to 1434 AH. He chaired a number of special committees and presented a number of proposals, such as the Housing Authority system and the Authority system Small and medium enterprises, the localization system for maintenance and operation contracts jobs, and a bonus proposal The issuance, the cadre of engineers, and others, in addition to a number of additional recommendations through the authorities’ reports Governmental.


- Engineer Al Guwaihes then assumed the position of Secretary of the Holy Capital, where he implemented and followed up several projects Projects and initiatives aimed at developing the Holy Capital.
